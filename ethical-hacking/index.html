Important Disclaimer

Ethical hacking means hacking with permission. Never test systems you don't own or have explicit written permission to test. Unauthorized hacking is illegal and can result in serious consequences.

Safe platforms: TryHackMe, HackTheBox, VulnHub, PentesterLab — all provide legal environments.
